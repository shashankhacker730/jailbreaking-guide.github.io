Blob Saving


You must have a Mac or Windows PC to continue with the guide.
For this guide you will need the following;
  • iOS Device with at least 600MB of free space
  • Charge & Sync Cable for your iOS Device
  • Mac or Windows PC
  • iTunes for Windows Users
Step 1: Getting the ECID
  1. Connect your iOS device to your Mac or PC (Apple OEM/MFi Certified cables are recommended)
  2. Open iTunes (Windows users will need to download it here) and click on the iPhone/iPad/iPod Touch icon
  3. This will show a summary of your iOS Device, click the Serial Number to reveal the ECID
  4. Save the ECID somewhere for later
    5. For iPhone 6S, iPhone 6S Plus, and iPhone SE devices, continue to Step 2. All other devices continue to Step 3.


Step 2: Getting the Board Configuration
  1. Install the app AX-CPU
  2. Open the app and look at the Model this is your board configuration save it for later.
Step 3: Saving blobs
  1. Open the TSSsaver with Safari
  2. Choose your device and check what iOS versions are signed
  3. Go back to TSSsaver and fill out the information

    4. If all of the information was inputted correctly, you should be directed to a plaintext page.

  4. Find the line of text along the lines of SHSH blobs will be saved in https://tsssaver.1conan.com/shsh/[REDACTED]
  5. After clicking on the link, click Download ZIP on the new tab.
You have now saved SHSH Blobs for your device and can continue to the Jailbreaking Guide.
