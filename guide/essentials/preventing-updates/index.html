Prevent Updates on iOS Devices

This method only works on iOS 11 and iOS 12 Devices. No alternatives exist at this time.
For this guide you will need the following;
  • iOS Device running iOS 11 or iOS 12
  • watchOS or tvOS Beta Profile (we can't link to this)
  • An Internet Connection
Step 1: Downloading the Profile
  1. Open up Safari, other browsers will not work.
  2. Download the tvOS or watchOS Beta Profile (we can't link to this).
  3. If you are on Safari, a dialog box "This website is trying to open Settings to show you a configuration profile. Do you want to allow this?" will show. Tap allow and the profile will begin to download
Step 2: Installing the Profile
  1. Once the profile has been downloaded, navigate to Settings > General > Profiles & Device Management.
  2. Tap "Install" for the Beta Profile.
  3. Follow all onscreen prompts.
  4. After the profile installs you will be prompting to restart.

Now all OTA updates from Apple will be supressed until you uninstall the profile.

You can now continue to the Jailbreaking Guide.
