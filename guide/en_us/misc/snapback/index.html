SnapBack

This method supports all devices running iOS 10.3 and newer.
Make sure your device is above 30% when restore RootFS Snapshots or above 50% when restoring Var Snapshots.
This guide requires your device to already be jailbroken on iOS 10.3 or newer. You can jailbreak your device using the multitude of guides here.
Step 1: Jailbreak
  1. Make sure you are jailbroken
    2. If you aren't jailbroken, check out our guides on Jailbreaking.
Step 2: Install SnapBack
  1. Open Cydia/Sileo and navigate to the Sources tab
  2. Tap Edit (top-right), then tap Add (top-left)
  3. Add the source https://repo.mindnightchips.me.com/
  4. Once Cydia/Sileo has finished reloading its sources, navigate to Midnightchips Repo > All Packages > SnapBack
  5. Tap Modify (top-right) then tap Install
  6. Confirm all the packages that need to be installed/upgraded
If you experience an error while trying to install SnapBack, make sure you are running the latest version of your Jailbreak and its utilities. If you continue to encounter further issues, consult the Help page.
Step 3: Make Snapshot
  1. Once Cydia/Sileo has finished installing SnapBack, open SnapBack
  2. Tap on the + icon in the top right to create a RootFS Snapshot
    3. If you want to create a Var Snapshot, navigate to the Var Snapshots tab.
    RootFS Snapshots may use around 1GB - 2GB of storage. Var Snapshots tend to be much larger in size and are only recommended for >=32GB devices.
Step 4: Jump to Snapshot
  • Open SnapBack and choose one of your RootFS and Var Snapshots
    • If you are looking to use a different jailbreak, jump to the backup labeled orig-fs.
  • Tap Jump to Snapshot to jump
    • Do not exit the app, put your device to sleep, or power off your device until SnapBack is done.
  • When prompted to reboot your device, hold the Sleep/Wake (Side) Button and Home Button to force a shut down.
    • On iPhone 7 and newer, you will need to hold one of the Volume Buttons to power off.
    Once you've rebooted, you will now be running on your restored snapshot. If you jumped to orig-fs, you can rejailbreak with a compatible jailbreak method.
