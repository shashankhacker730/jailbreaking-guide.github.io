Saving Blobs


This guide has two sections. Saving Blobs can be performed on either your iOS Device itself or using a Computer.

Step 1A: Downloading TSS Saver (iOS)
Using the TSS Saver App requires a jailbreak. If you are not jailbroken, find a guide pertaining to your device and iOS version
  1. Add https://repo.nullpixel.uk/ repo your sources
  2. Download and Install TSS Saver
Step 2A: Saving Blobs with TSS Saver
  1. Open TSS Saver
    2. If the app crashes upon opening, you may not be jailbroken and will need to rejailbreak.
  2. Tap Save Blobs
  3. Once you receive the confirmation message, tap Open
    4. It may take some time for new blobs to appear especially if the server is under load.
    You have now saved blobs on your iOS Device! The rest of this guide is for those saving blobs with a computer. Please share this guide with others and join our Discord Server.
Step 1B: Getting the ECID (Computer)
  1. Connect your iOS device to your Mac or PC (Apple OEM/MFi Certified cables are recommended)
  2. Open iTunes (Windows users will need to download it here) and click on the iPhone/iPad/iPod Touch icon
  3. This will show a summary of your iOS Device, click the Serial Number to reveal the ECID
  4. Save the ECID somewhere for later
    5. For iPhone 6S, iPhone 6S Plus, and iPhone SE devices, continue to Step 2. All other devices continue to Step 3.


Step 2B: Getting the Board Configuration
  1. Install the app AX-CPU
  2. Open the app and look at the Model this is your board configuration save it for later.
Step 3B: Saving blobs
  1. Open the TSSsaver with Safari
  2. Choose your device and check what iOS versions are signed
  3. Go back to TSSsaver and fill out the information

    4. If all of the information was inputted correctly, you should be directed to a plaintext page.

  4. You will get a message along the lines of
    5. SHSH blobs will be saved in https://tsssaver.1conan.com/shsh/[REDACTED]
  5. After clicking on the link, click Download ZIP on the new tab.
You have now saved SHSH Blobs for your device and can continue to the Jailbreaking Guide.
