Sideloading

Before continuing this guide, please make sure you have backed up your iOS Device via iTunes and iCloud.
AltStore
    On Windows, make sure to install iTunes (not UWP) and iCloud
  1. Download and install AltStore
    2. On macOS, you will need to click on the AltStore icon in the Menu Bar and then click Install Mail Plug-in. Afterwards, open the Mail app and open preferences. Navigate to General > Manage Preferences and check Alstore and restart the Mail app.
  2. Connect your iOS Device to your Mac or PC via USB
  3. Click on AltStore in the Menu Bar (macOS) or AltServer in the System Tray (Windows) and login with your Apple ID.
  4. On your iPhone, navigate to Settings > General > Device Management > (AppleID) and trust AltStore.
Now, you can open any IPA file you want in AltStore to sign it on your iOS device.
