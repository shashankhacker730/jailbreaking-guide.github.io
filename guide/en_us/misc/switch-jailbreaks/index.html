Switch Jailbreaks

Make sure your device is sufficiently charged.
Unc0ver -> Chimera/Electra
Switching to Chimera/Electra will cause you to lose your installed tweaks.
  1. Open Unc0ver
  2. Navigate to the Settings tab and disable all settings
  3. Enable Restore RootFS
  4. Navigate to the main tab and tap Restore RootFS
You can now follow our Chimera or Electra Jailbreak Guide.
Electra/Chimera -> Unc0ver
You should be able to go directly from Electra/Chimera to Unc0ver. This will erase all of your installed tweaks.
  1. Follow our Unc0ver Jailbreak Guide
Unc0ver -> checkra1n
Switching between Unc0ver and checkra1n is incredibly easy.
  1. Make an empty file at root .mount_rw
You can now switch between Unc0ver and checkra1n freely without losing your tweaks.
Chimera -> checkra1n
  1. Open the Chimera app
  2. Scroll down and tap Restore RootFS
  3. Jailbreak with checkra1n (guide coming soon)
