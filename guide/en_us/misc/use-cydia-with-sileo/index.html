Install Cydia Alongside Sileo

This guide assumes you are already jailbroken. If you would like to jailbreak your iOS Device, our Unc0ver Dark, Chimera, or Electra guides are a good place to start.
Step 1: Cydia Installation (unc0ver Dark)
    Make sure you are on the latest version of unc0ver dark. You can download the latest version here.
  1. Open the unc0ver dark app and navigate to the Settings tab
  2. Enable the settings (Re)install Sileo and Reinstall Cydia
  3. Navigate back to the Jailbreak tab and tap Jailbreak.
    4. Do not lock or shutdown your device during the Jailbreak process.
  4. Once your device is done installing Cydia and Sileo, you can respring (if required).
Upon respringing, you will now have both Cydia and Sileo installed and they can be used alongside each other! Don't forget to share this guide with your friends and join the Jailbreak Guide Discord Server.
Step 1: Cydia Installation (Chimera/Electra)
    Chimera users should be using v1.2.7. You can download it from here.
  1. Open Sileo and navigate to the Sources tab
  2. Add the source https://diatr.us/chicydia/ to your list of sources
  3. Once Sileo has refreshed its sources, you can install the Cydia for Chimera/Electra package
    4. You can also (optionally) install Remove Sileo to remove Sileo from Chimera or Electra
  4. Once the installation has finished, you need to reboot your iOS Device and rejailbreak.
    5. You have now successfully installed Cydia alongside Sileo. Don't forget to share this guide with your friends and join the Jailbreak Guide Discord Server.
