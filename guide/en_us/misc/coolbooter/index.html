CoolBooter

This method supports iPhone 4, iPhone 4S, iPhone 5, iPhone 5c, iPad 2, iPad 3, iPad 4, iPad Mini 1G, and iPod Touch 5G on iOS 5 - iOS 10 (in our testing)
Before continuing this guide, please make sure you have backed up your iOS Device via iTunes and iCloud.
Make sure to keep your device charged while following this guide as the dualbooting process does take some time.
This guide requires your device to already be jailbroken on iOS 5 - iOS 10. You can jailbreak your device using the multitude of guides here.
Step 1: Jailbreak
  1. Make sure you are jailbroken
  2. If you are using >=9.1, make sure your jailbreak has been kickstarted
Step 2: Install CoolBooter
  1. Open Cydia and navigate to the Sources tab
  2. Tap Edit (top-right), then tap Add (top-left)
  3. Add the source https://coolbooter.com/
  4. Once Cydia has finished reloading its sources, navigate to CoolBooter Repo > All Packages > CoolBooter
  5. Tap Modify (top-right) then tap Install
  6. Confirm all the packages that need to be installed/upgrade
If you experience an error while trying to install CoolBooter, try rebooting and trying again. If you continue to encounter further issues, consult the Troubleshooting page.
Step 3: Install Other iOS
  1. Once Cydia has finished installing CoolBooter, open CoolBooter
  2. Tap on Install and choose your version of iOS
    3. You are free to choose any version of iOS from 5.0 - 9.3.5. You can also tap Storage to customize the amount of storage the new partition will use.
  3. Once you've selected an iOS version, tap I'm Ready.
    4. You will now be guided through a few prompts. All of these are optional. We recommend you choose to jailbreak the new OS.
    You will now be prompted for Data Protection Workaround (BETA). If you do not know what this is, choose No.
    Now that you have completed all prompts, CoolBooter will begin to download and install your selected version of iOS.
  4. When the installation has concluded, reboot your iOS Device.
    5. You will need to Kickstart your iOS Device if you are running anything above iOS 9.0.2
Step 4: Boot Into Other iOS
  • Open CoolBooter and the option to Boot should be available
  • Tap Boot
  • When prompted to lock your device, lock it
    • You have no successfully CoolBooted your iOS Device.
    To boot back into your original iOS version, just reboot your device. Booting into the second version of iOS is as easy as running CoolBooter again.
