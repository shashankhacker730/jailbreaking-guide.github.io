Cydia Impactor Errors

Cydia Impactor says I need an "app password". What is that?
If you have Two-Factor Authentication enabled on your Apple ID, you may need to create an app password to use with Cydia Impactor.
App passwords can be reused after they are created, but may expire after some time.
  1. Login to your Apple Account at appleid.apple.com
  2. Navigate to the Security section and click on Generate App-Specific Password
  3. Follow all onscreen prompts to create your App Password
  4. You can now paste this unique password into Cydia Impactor
