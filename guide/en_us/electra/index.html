Electra

This method only supports All Devices on iOS 11.x.x . For iOS >=12.0, check out Chimera, Unc0ver.
If you would like to install Cydia, you can follow this guide.
For this guide you will need the following;
  • The Electra iPA
  • Cydia Impactor
Step 1: Impactor
  1. Connect your iOS device to your Mac or PC (Apple OEM cables are recommended)
  2. Start Cydia Impactor, then drag the Electra IPA onto the window
  3. You will be prompted for your Apple ID credentials (This can be the apple account on your phone. Make sure the password is correct)
  4. Once you've entered your credentials in correctly, Cydia Impactor should install Electra onto your iOS Device. If you get an error checkhere
If this fails, try again with your correct Apple ID or a different charge/sync cable or go to the Troubleshooting section.
Step 2: Using Electra
  1. Once the Electra App has been installed onto your iOS Device, make sure to delete all OTA updates if any
  2. Open Settings then navigate to General > Profiles & Device Management. The Apple ID used to install the app should show.
  3. Tap on the Profile, then tap Verify (or Trust) and follow all prompts onscreen
  4. Now that Electra has been verified, open it on your Home Screen
  5. Once open, tap Jailbreak and follow the onscreen prompts.
During the Jailbreaking process, you may encounter;
  • A respring on 2/3
  • Random respring
  • Various other errors
These errors are normal. If you encounter any of these, reboot your device and try again.
Step 3: Cydia
  1. After your device resprings, you should have Cydia installed on your Home Screen
  2. If Cydia is not installed, repeat Step 2 again. If you encounter further issues refer to the Troubleshooting section.
  3. Launch Cydia and let it (re)load its packages. If you are prompted for an upgrade, tap Upgrade All
  4. Using Cydia alongside Sileo in Chimera and Electra is now available here!

Once all upgrades are completed and you've resprung, you are free to use any (compatible) Cydia tweaks

Also feel free to check out the Jailbreaking Essentials
If cydia and/or any tweaks you have aren't working check Troubleshooting page. Make sure the tweak is compatible for your ios version before hand.
