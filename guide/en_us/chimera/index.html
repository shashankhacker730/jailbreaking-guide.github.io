Chimera

This method only supports All Devices on iOS 12 - 12.4 and some iOS 12.3 Developer Betas (not Beta 6)
Before proceeding with this guide, there are some things you will need to consider;
  • There is no A12 Support on >=12.1.3
  • The current exploit for iOS 12.4 only supports A9 - A11 devices
  • All iOS 12.3 Developer Betas are compatible up to Beta 5
  • Using Cydia alongside Sileo in Chimera and Electra is now available here!
Cydia Impactor and programs like it are having issues signing apps. Chimera has its own OTA signing service available on chimera.sh
For this guide you will need the following;
  • The Chimera IPA corresponding to your iOS Version
  • Cydia Impactor
    or
  • Chimera.sh OTA Signing Service
Step 1: Impactor
  1. Connect your iOS device to your Mac or PC (Apple OEM cables are recommended)
  2. Start Cydia Impactor, then drag the Chimera IPA onto the window
  3. You will be prompted for your Apple ID credentials (This can be the apple account on your phone. Make sure the password is correct)
  4. Once you've entered your credentials in correctly, Cydia Impactor should install Chimera onto your iOS Device. If you get an error check here
If this fails, try again with your correct Apple ID or a different charge/sync cable or go to the Troubleshooting section.
Step 2: Using Chimera
  1. Once the Chimera App has been installed onto your iOS Device, make sure to delete all OTA updates if any
  2. Open Settings then navigate to General > Profiles & Device Management. The Apple ID used to install the app should show.
  3. Tap on the Profile, then tap Verify (or Trust) and follow all prompts onscreen
  4. Now that Chimera has been verified, open it on your Home Screen
  5. Once open, tap Jailbreak and follow the onscreen prompts, if any are present.
During the Jailbreaking process, you may encounter;
  • Random respring
  • Various other errors
These errors are normal. If you encounter any of these, reboot your device and try again.
Step 3: Sileo
  1. After your device resprings, you should have Sileo installed on your Home Screen
  2. If Sileo is not installed, repeat Step 2 again. If you encounter further issues refer to the Troubleshooting section.
  3. Launch Sileo and let it (re)load its packages. If you are prompted for an upgrade, tap Upgrade All

Once all upgrades are completed and you've resprung, you are free to use any (compatible) Sileo tweaks

Also feel free to check out the Jailbreaking Essentials
If Sileo and/or any tweaks you have aren't working check Troubleshooting page. Make sure the tweak is compatible for your ios version before hand.
