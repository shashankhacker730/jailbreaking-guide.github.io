Totally Not Spyware

This method only supports 64-Bit Devices on iOS 10.X. If you have a 32-Bit device use H3lix. If this Jailbreak does not work for you, try Double H3lix.
This guide hasn't gotten very much love. If you would like to contribute, click here.
Step 1: Totally Not Spyware Jailbreak
Do NOT run this on an unsupported device as this may cause issues.
  1. Open this website on your iPhone with Safari.
  2. Slide Slide for Spyware to Jailbreak
If you get an error, try again. This may take a few tries.
Step 2: Cydia
  1. After your device reboots, you should have Cydia installed on your Home Screen
  2. Launch Cydia and let it (re)load its packages. If you are prompted for an upgrade, tap Upgrade All

Once all upgrades are completed and you've resprung, you are free to use any (compatible) Cydia tweaks.
