Unc0ver

This method only supports devices on iOS 11.0 - 13.3 A12 and A13 only support 13.3 If you would like to have Sileo support, use Chimera.
Before proceeding with this guide, there are some things you will need to consider;
  • A12 and A13 support for iOS 13 + A11 and under will get support later
  • A11 and under are supported on all iOS using checkra1n
Cydia Impactor and Signing Services have been having issues, find out how to sign apps here
For this guide you will need the following;
  • Unc0ver IPA
  • A way to sideload/sign the U0 IPA
Step 1: Sideload App
This section of the guide has been ommitted due to isseus with Cydia Impactor. To learn how to sideload an .ipa, visit our Sideloading Guide.
Step 2: Using Unc0ver
  1. Once the Unc0ver App has been installed onto your iOS Device, make sure to delete all OTA updates if any
  2. Open Settings then navigate to General > Profiles & Device Management. The Apple ID used to install the app should show.
  3. Tap on the Profile, then tap Verify (or Trust) and follow all prompts onscreen
  4. Now that Unc0ver has been verified, open it on your Home Screen
  5. Once open, tap Jailbreak and follow the onscreen prompts.
During the Jailbreaking process, you may encounter;
  • Random Respring or Reboot
  • Various other errors
These errors are normal. If you encounter any of these, reboot your device and try again. You can also consult the Troubleshooting section.
Step 3: Cydia
  1. After your device resprings, you should have Cydia installed on your Home Screen
    • If Cydia is not installed, repeat Step 2 again. If you encounter further issues refer to the Troubleshooting section.
  2. Launch Cydia and let it (re)load its packages. If you are prompted for an upgrade, tap Upgrade All

Once all upgrades have been completed and you've resprung, you are free to use your Jailbreak to your heart's content.
