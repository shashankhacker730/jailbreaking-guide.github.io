Removing a Jailbreak

Removing a Jailbreak is a must if you plan on turning in your phone for an upgrade to your carrier.
Jailbroken iOS Devices fetch for more money on sites like eBay.
Cydia Eraser
Cydia Eraser is only compatible with iOS 7.1 - 10.3.3
For users running iOS 7.1 - 10.3.3, you can download and install Cydia Eraser through Cydia.
SemiRestore11 & rec0ver
For use only on iOS 11 - 12.1.2 with Electra/Chimera, unc0ver, and unc0ver dark Jailbreaks.
When you Jailbreak with unc0ver, unc0ver dark, or Electra/Chimera, a Snapshot will be created of your original RootFS. By selecting the 'Restore RootFS' option in unc0ver and Chimera, you can restore your iOS device to this stock backup. Some remnants of your jailbreak will remain, but they will not effect anything.

If you are using Electra11, download and install SemiRestore11 to remove your jailbreak.
iTunes Restore
In most cases, this is not needed to remove a Jailbreak.
If all else fails, you can restore your iOS device using iTunes on a Mac or PC.
